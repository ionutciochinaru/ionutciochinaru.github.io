Frequently Asked Questions

What does “Promoted jobs in search” mean?

This means your job posts can be seen first on a consultant’s job feed or in job search results.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can switch between plans at any time. Choose a plan from the Subscription section in your account side menu.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel any monthly paid plan (Silver, Gold, Platinum) by selecting the Standard plan. This will take effect once your latest monthly paid subscription expires. You can upgrade to another plan at any time.

What support options are available?

Open a live conversation with inctrl support directly in Chat, or write us an email at office@inctrl.ai .
